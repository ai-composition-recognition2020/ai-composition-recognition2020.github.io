is gradually emerging, and more and more artificial intelligence composition technology is introduced into the commercial field, which has also caused a series of potential intellectual property disputes and thinking about the intellectual property rights infringement identification standards and fact determination procedures, such as: For the misappropriation of artificial intelligence composition algorithms, even the artificial infringement of music melody is attributed to the algorithm defects of artificial intelligence composition system, the objective degree of subjective judgment of natural persons, and the degree of dependence of litigation procedures on professional identification. Introduction

The goal of this challenge is to identify single-melody music generated by artificial intelligence. The project uses two databases with distinct styles to train three artificial neural network automatic composition systems with distinct working principles, and then generates a certain number of artificial intelligence music melody according to the obtained six models. The music is in MIDI format , Contestants use algorithms to classify music.

Training Dataset (for generation system)

This competition uses two styles of music to train the model separately. The two styles are pop music and Bach music in the Baroque period. Among the popular music, only the melody part is used, and Bach's music uses the melody of each voice as a single melody.

子任务 A：判别特定旋律是否由人工智能算法生成

该任务的目的是辨别人工智能生成的旋律与人类创作的旋律，数据集中包含了两种风格的旋律， 流行音乐及巴洛克时期巴赫的音乐，任何一种风格的音乐都包含人工智能生成的及人类创作的， 参赛者需要将人工智能生成的旋律及人类创作的旋律进行分类， 比赛以分类准确率作为评判标准。

开发数据集

开发数据集包含了4000个MIDI文件，每个文件为单旋律音乐，曲速为120bpm， 每首旋律长度为8小节，其中2000首为人工智能算法生成，2000首来自原有数据库。 对于人工智能生成的音乐，使用流行音乐和巴赫音乐的数据库，分别对三种自动作曲系统进行训练得到六种模型。 使用六种模型各生成一定数量的旋律组成2000首。 对于人类的音乐，随机在两种风格的数据库中挑选一部分旋律，并切分8小节长度（该8小节并不一定为完整乐句）， 曲速调整为120bpm，组成2000首。 其中人工智能旋律与原有数据库中旋律有对应的标签，注意数据集中也会对不同风格的音乐进行标注， 但此任务不需要对风格进行分类。 开发数据包含2000首MIDI文件及一个csv文件，MIDI文件以下列方式命名。 

[composer label]-[style]-[id].mid

csv文件给出了对应文件的标签如下表所示，composer label中“0”对应的是人工智能作曲，“1”对应的是人类作曲。

file name style composer label
0-bach-0.mid bach 0
0-bach-1.mid bach 0
1-pop-0.mid pop 1
1-pop-1.mid pop 1

子任务 B：判别人工智能算法生成的旋律属于哪种生成算法

该任务的目标是辨别人工智能生成的旋律属于三种自动作曲系统中的哪一种， 将三种系统生成的两种风格的旋律混合在一起，每一个系统生成的旋律有相应的标签。 参赛者需要将旋律分成三类，每一类对应一个自动作曲系统。比赛以分类准确率作为评判标准。

开发数据集

开发数据集包含了3000个MIDI文件，均为人工智能生成。每个文件为单旋律音乐，曲速为120bpm， 每首旋律长度为8小节，其中1000首为系统A生成；1000首为系统B生成；1000首为系统C生成 不同系统生成的旋律标记有对应的标签。 开发数据包含3000首MIDI文件及一个csv文件，MIDI文件以下列方式命名。 

[system label]-[style]-[id].mid
csv文件给出了对应文件的标签如下表所示， system label中“A”对应的为生成系统A，“B”对应的为生成系统B，“C”对应的为生成系统C。

file name style system label
A-bach-0.mid bach A
B-bach-1.mid bach B
C-bach-2.mid bach C
C-pop-0.mid pop C

提交

参赛者可以选择仅参加一个子任务，或者同时参加两个子任务

参赛者需要提交一个打包的zip文件，该zip包括：

• 系统的输出文件（*.csv）

• 对系统作出详细解释的技术报告（*.pdf）

• 可以运行的完整系统（放在单独的文件夹中）

系统输出文件应为单个文件（CSV格式，带有标题行），其中包含评估数据集中 每一个MIDI文件的分类结果，并且结果需要包含每个标签的输出概率值。

提交的详细信息请查看提交页面。

External data resources

Use of external data is allowed under the following conditions:

• The used external resource is clearly referenced and freely accessible to any other research group in the world. External data refers to public datasets or trained models. The data must be public and freely available before 15th of August 2020.

• Participants submit at least one system without external training data so that we can study the contribution of such resources. This condition applies only to cases where external audio datasets are used. In case of external data being pretrained models or embeddings, this condition does not apply.

• The list of external data sources used in training must be clearly indicated in the technical report.

• Participants inform the organizers in advance about such data sources, so that all competitors know about them and have equal opportunity to use them. Please send and email to the task coordinators; we will update the list of external datasets on the webpage accordingly. Once the evaluation set is published, the list of allowed external data resources is locked (no further external sources allowed).

Task rules

There are general rules for participants:

• Use of external data is allowed. See conditions for external data usage here。

• Manipulation of provided training and development data is allowed.

• Participants are not allowed to make subjective judgments of the evaluation data, nor to annotate it. The evaluation dataset cannot be used to train the submitted system.

• Classification decision must be done independently for each test sample.

Baseline system

The baseline system will be released at the same time when the development dataset is released.
